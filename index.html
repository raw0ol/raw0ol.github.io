About Me

Hi there! I am a self-taught software engineer. Prior to transitioning into tech, I worked as a physical therapist in the healthcare industry.

While I enjoyed helping patients improve their mobility and overall quality of life, I became interested in the ways in which technology could further enhance the healthcare industry. I began teaching myself software development on my free time and eventually made the decision to pursue a career in tech.

One of the things that drew me to software engineering is the ability to continuously learn and grow. In my previous role as a physical therapist, I developed strong communication and problem-solving skills, which have translated well to my current career in tech. I believe that soft skills, such as the ability to work well in a team and adapt to new challenges, are just as important as technical skills in this field.
